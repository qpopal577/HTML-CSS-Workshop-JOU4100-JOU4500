Adapt to your surroundings

A good photographer adapts to his or her surroundings. In some scenarios the light will be poor, you won't be able to use flash etc. Do the best you can to take the best photo you can. Get up close and personal -to the extent that it's possible- and remember the age-old adage: "It's better to ask forgiveness than permission."
